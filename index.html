About

Nike je globalno priznana ameriška blagovna znamka za športno opremo, ustanovljena leta 1964. Podjetje se specializira za športno obutev, oblačila in opremo ter slovi po inovacijah, visoki kakovosti izdelkov ter prepoznavnem logotipu "Swoosh". Nike sodeluje z znanimi športniki, ustvarjalci mode in izvaja različne družbene pobude. Je eno največjih imen v svetu športne industrije z globalno prisotnostjo.

Phil Knight

CEO & Founder

Phil Knight, born on February 24, 1938, is an American business magnate and co-founder of Nike, Inc., one of the world's leading and most recognizable sportswear and athletic shoe companies.

Nike Swoosh

Logo

The Nike logo, known as the "Swoosh," is one of the most iconic and recognizable symbols in the world.The Swoosh became the emblem of the brand, associated with a spirit of victory and athletic determination.
